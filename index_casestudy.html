CINCH CASE STUDY

Cinch

A travel app designed for people on the go.We designed this app with COVID restrictions and protocols in mind.

IDEATION AND DESIGN

We reiterated on our user persona after our research findings to determine what functionality and options our apps should include.

USABILITY AND TESTING

After conducting our usability tests, we were able to determine what kind of functions our target demographic would want to make this app successful.

PROTOTYPE

We conducted a competitor analysis of similar apps already on the market and based our user flow on our useability test and ease of use. We then sketched our wireframes and created our prototype.

FINDINGS AND CONCLUSION

In this research project, we planned and conducted 5 interviews with the target demographic of busy professionals who travel a lot for work and personal reasons in the Washington, DC area.
